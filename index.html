Fun fact

Kai-awase (貝合わせ) is a traditional shell game with origins in Japan. The interior of the shells featured
elaborate paintings, often representing scenes from traditional Japanese literature. The aim of the game
was to find the other half that would fit. Many games were later developed based on it.

How To Play Memory Match

  1. Press the start button to start the timer and begin a new game.
  2. Choose two cards to flip over at a time and try to remember the images.
  3. When you flip two cards, they will be checked for matches. If they match, the cards will be removed from the board, and you continue with the remaining cards.
  4. Keep selecting and flipping two cards at a time, trying to find matches until all the cards are cleared from the board.
  5. Once all the cards have been matched, you win the game.
  6. The timer will stop, and you will be shown the score page.
